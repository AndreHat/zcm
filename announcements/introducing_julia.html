Announcement: Introducing Julia Support

Hi All,

Wanted to let you all know that we've just added support for Julia (v0.6.4)! For those of you who aren't familiar with Julia, Julia is a high performance dynamically-typed language that is very useful for numerical computing. With ZCM Julia integration, you can now use Julia to interact directly with ZCM traffic and ZCM logs. For more information on Julia, check out https://julialang.org/

Examples that leverage the various parts of the ZCM Julia API are provided in the examples/julia subdirectory

As always, feel free to reach out on our forums or on gitter with any questions!
